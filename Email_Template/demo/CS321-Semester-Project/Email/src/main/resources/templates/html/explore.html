Email Writing Guide

Parts of an Email:

  1. Subject Line: A concise summary of your email's purpose.
  2. Greeting: Start with a friendly or professional salutation, such as "Dear [Name]" or "Hi [Name]."
  3. Body:
    • Introduction: Briefly introduce the purpose of your email.
    • Details: Provide the main content or details relevant to your message.
    • Call to Action: Specify what you want the recipient to do next.
  4. Closing: Conclude politely with a phrase like "Thank you for your time."
  5. Signature: Include your name and contact information.
